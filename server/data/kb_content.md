# Do you charge any fee for Atome card?

**Category:** Atome Card  
**Section:** Card feature  
**URL:** https://help.atome.ph/hc/en-gb/articles/8713025969689-Do-you-charge-any-fee-for-Atome-card  
**Last Updated:** 2025-05-08T03:46:11Z

---

We do not charge any annual fees, and no extra charges will be added if you make your payments on time. However, if you decide to split your Atome Card bill into 3 or 6 equal monthly payments, interest fees and transaction processing fees may be applied on any outstanding amount, which will be clearly outlined on your bill statement.

To better understand our fees and charges, here's a helpful breakdown:

**Late Fee**: 5% of the outstanding amount, excluding accrued interest or late fee, calculated on a monthly basis.

**Card Replacement Fee**: A flat rate of ₱400 per card.

**Foreign Exchange Conversion Fee**: 3.10% of the transaction amount (includes a 1.98% service fee and a 1.12% cross-border processing fee levied by MasterCard).

**Repayment Processing Fee**: Up to 2.24% of the payment amount (including tax), with a minimum fee of ₱1.

**Transaction Processing Fee**: This ranges from 0% to 2.24% of the transaction amount (including tax).

**Loan Restructure Fee:** This ranges from 0% to 3.10% of the restructured loan amount.

You may find more information under the Terms & Conditions and Loan Agreement, accessible via the Atome app.

# Help! I think I may have an unauthorized transaction. 

**Category:** Atome Card  
**Section:** Transaction and refund  
**URL:** https://help.atome.ph/hc/en-gb/articles/12772455290649-Help-I-think-I-may-have-an-unauthorized-transaction  
**Last Updated:** 2025-02-13T10:26:20Z

---

Here are some guidelines to help you.

1) Please lock the affected card via the Atome App > “Atome Card” > “Lock” instantly to prevent any further transactions. If you do not have access to Atome App, please contact us to help right away.

2) These are some common scenarios are often mistaken for unauthorized charges:

a) Family and friends: Check if any of your family members or friends who have access to your card or account had made a purchase.

b) Canceled orders: If you have cancelled an order recently, the transaction may still appear as a pending charge for the time being. It will disappear after a short period of time once the cancellation is completely processed.

c) Duplicate charges: A duplicate charge may result in transactions appearing as a pending charge. It will disappear after a short period of time, once the voided order has been completely processed. If you were charged twice for the same transaction, double check your payment subscriptions and check your orders for duplicates.

d) Recently added payment methods: When you add a new card as a payment method, you might see a pending charge of a small amount in your transactions. This is a temporary authorization you won’t be charged for.

3) If you’ve confirmed that the transaction was not authorized and has been billed (ie. not pending), please contact customer service to file a transaction dispute request. Atome shall diligently endeavor to advise the final resolution of the reported dispute within sixty (60) calendar days from the date Atome acknowledges request of the reported dispute.

Note: If the dispute is proved to be invalid, the transaction will remain on your bill, along with the following fees:

1. An invalid dispute fee of P1,000 for every invalid case/transaction

2. MasterCard charges (filing fee, administrative fee, etc)

3. Corresponding finance charge and late payment fee

For example (excluding the origination fee for illustration):

Your receiving disbursement amount will be:

₱1,000

Your monthly repayment amount will be:

(₱1,000 + ₱1,000\*2%\*6 + ₱1,000\*3%\*6 + ₱1,000\*1%\*6)/6 = ₱226.67

Transparency matters to us. All fees will be clearly displayed in your **Repayment Plan** and **Loan Agreement** before confirmation.

# How is the interest calculated on my Atome Savings account?

**Category:** Atome Savings  
**Section:** Interest Rate, Fees & Charges  
**URL:** https://help.atome.ph/hc/en-gb/articles/37618434191257-How-is-the-interest-calculated-on-my-Atome-Savings-account  
**Last Updated:** 2025-06-12T02:27:06Z

---

Interest is calculated daily based on your end-of-day balance, between 12:00 AM and 1:00 AM, and is credited to your account every day. A 20% withholding tax is applied separately, and both the gross interest earned and the tax deduction will be displayed as separate line items in your transaction history.

Here’s how the interest is calculated:  
Daily interest = End-of-day balance × interest rate × (1/365)

Good to know:  
- For new accounts, interest starts accruing 24 hours after your first deposit is made.  
- No interest is earned on the day of activation.  
- On the second day, your interest credit is calculated based on the average end-of-day balance of the first two days.  
- From the third day onwards, interest is calculated daily based on your end-of-day balance.

**Category:** Atome Savings  
**Section:** Deposit & Fund Transfer  
**URL:** https://help.atome.ph/hc/en-gb/articles/37618950218265-How-can-I-deposit-funds-to-my-Atome-Savings-account  
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-11T16:00:02Z

---

You can conveniently deposit funds into your Atome Savings account through the following methods:

1. **Deposit to Atome Savings via Online Bank / E-Wallet transfer via PESONet or InstaPay, or direct transfer from another Netbank account:**

   1. Open **Atome Savings,** tap Deposit, then tap Transfer via Account.
   2. Copy your Netbank Account Number and Netbank Account Name
   3. Once copied, head to your preferred local bank or e-wallet app. Tap Send Money or Transfer Money
   4. Select **Netbank Rural Bank, Inc. (Netbank)** as your destination bank.
   5. Paste your Netbank Account Number and Netbank Account Name in the appropriate fields. Then, include your desired deposit amount.
   6. Complete your transaction
   7. Wait to receive a confirmation via push, in-app, and/or email once the funds are successfully deposited.
2. **Deposit to Atome Savings via InstaPay QR**

   1. Open **Atome Savings,** tap Deposit, then tap Transfer via QR
   2. Save the QR code to your device
   3. Open your bank or e-wallet app
   4. Scan the QR code in your app
   5. Confirm transfer
   6. Wait to receive a confirmation via push, in-app, and/or email once the funds are successfully deposited.

Transfer fees may apply. Transferring from a Netbank account to your Atome Savings is free.

PESONet transfers may take 1-2 banking days to process. InstaPay and PESONet transfers may also be subject to limits set by your bank.

# I transferred funds, but they were not reflected in the account.

**Category:** Atome Savings  
**Section:** Deposit & Fund Transfer  
**URL:** https://help.atome.ph/hc/en-gb/articles/37619044551065-I-transferred-funds-but-they-were-not-reflected-in-the-account  
**Last Updated:** 2025-06-12T02:28:37Z

---

Transaction postings can sometimes be delayed depending on the cut-off times and transfer method used (e.g., PESONet, InstaPay). If your transaction isn’t showing up in Latest Transactions, try refreshing the page after an hour. You can also select “Last 3 Months” from the dropdown menu to update the transaction list.

Here’s how different transfers are handled:

1. Netbank to Netbank transfers:  
Transfers between Netbank accounts should reflect immediately.  
  
2. InstaPay transfers:  
InstaPay transactions are also reflected instantly.  
  
3. PESONet transfers:  
PESONet transactions follow specific cut-off times:  
Before 10:00 AM on a business day → Processed starting 10:40 AM on the same day.  
10:01 AM – 1:00 PM on a business day → Processed starting 1:40 PM on the same day.  
1:01 PM – 4:00 PM on a business day → Processed starting 4:40 PM on the same day.  
After 4:00 PM or during weekends → Processed starting 10:40 AM on the next business day.

Example: If you made a transfer on a Friday after 4:00 PM or over the weekend, it will be reflected by Monday at 10:40 AM at the earliest. We suggest waiting until the end of the day to check your account balance. You can also contact the recipient bank as their posting times may vary.

# What if I enter the wrong recipient's details? Can I cancel or undo the transfer?

**Last Updated:** 2025-06-12T02:29:30Z

---

We understand mistakes can happen! While we recommend double-checking the recipient details before confirming your transfer, once the transaction is successfully processed, it cannot be cancelled or undone.

If you entered an incorrect account number for InstaPay or PESONet, don’t worry. The amount will be deducted initially and, if the transfer is rejected, it will automatically be reversed as a “Deposit.” Both the deduction and the refund will appear clearly in your transaction history.

# How can I pay my Atome Card or Cash bills using my Atome Savings account?

**Category:** Atome Savings  
**Section:** Deposit & Fund Transfer  
**URL:** https://help.atome.ph/hc/en-gb/articles/38417632489625-How-can-I-pay-my-Atome-Card-or-Cash-bills-using-my-Atome-Savings-account  
**Last Updated:** 2024-10-03T06:01:52Z

---

How to make your first Atome Card or Cash bill payment using your Atome Savings account:  
  
1. Open the "Bill Payment" section for Atome Card or Atome Cash in the Atome app.

2. Tap on "More Payment Methods" under the available payment options.

3. Select "Atome Savings" and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the payment.

4. Once successful, you'll receive a confirmation notification in the app or via email.  
  
For future payments, Atome Savings will appear under "Available Method(s)".
